Lingmell House B&B

Situated in the very heart of the Wasdale valley with views of Great Gable and Wastwater.

Lingmell House provides great quality B&B with Twin, Double & Single rooms recently refurbished to a very high standard. Ideal for walking & climbing, dogs welcome.

Open all year round, this former vicarage is perfectly situated for those who wish to walk from the front door with Great Gable, Scafell Pike and the Pillar range all in very close proximity.

Lingmell House B&B conforms to the Lake District National Park peace and quiet policy. Please note that TV and radio reception is not available
