Wasdale Webcam Feed


View our webcam feed from Wasdale Head here. Images are updated hourly.

Note that we have chosen to focus on Great Gable (on the right, Kirkfell is on the left). This is probably the most recognisable view but often makes the weather seem worse than it really is because Gable is frequently covered in cloud when most of the surrounding area is clear.

If you are unsure about the weather then the links below may be helpful.
