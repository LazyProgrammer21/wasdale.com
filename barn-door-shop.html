The barn Door Shop

Situated at the head of the Wasdale valley in the very heart of the English Lake District the Barn Door Shop offers everything the walker, climber & camper could need. From boots to waterproofs, maps to ice axes, we stock an extensive quality range of clothes & equipment which we are happy to demonstrate and advise upon
The shop is owned and run by keen and experienced mountaineers who are always happy to advise on clothing, equipment and routes
The Campsite, formerly run from the shop, has been operated by the Inn since March 2012

All you need for walking the Fells in and around Wasdale

We stock a huge range of both technical and non technical walking and climbing clothing and equipment. For the first time visitor to Wasdale who is hoping to climb England's highest peak we stock low budget boots and waterproofs, allowing you to hike up Scafell Pike or any of the surrounding fells. We also stock both mid-range and more technical products.
We are stockists for Paramo, Lowa, Aku, Petzl, Leki, Sprayway, Jack Wolfskin, Regatta, OMM, Weirdfish and many more

What we offer!

Books and Maps, Gifts and Cards
We stock a large range of books including local guides to Cumbria , the UK and international rock & trekking guides, a comprehensive instructional range and a truly huge selection of mountaineering based literature.
We also have a selection of gifts for anyone wanting a souvenir of their visit to Wasdale.
